Offers

Beginner's Package

Enjoy a series of three private coaching sessions with experienced surf instructors who will guide you through the basics of surfing and help you build confidence in the water. Additionally, you'll have access to top-quality surfboards and wetsuits for the duration of the sessions.

Surfing Safari

Join our experienced surf guides as they take you to the best surf breaks in the region. Each day, you'll visit a different spot known for its unique waves and scenery. Transportation, lunch, and professional guiding are included.

Surf Member

With the Surfboard Membership, you can choose from our wide selection of high-quality surfboards whenever you feel like hitting the waves. No need to worry about storage, upkeep, or transporting your own board. It's all taken care of for you.
